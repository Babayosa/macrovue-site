Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 8, 2026

Overview

Macrovue is designed with privacy as a core principle. We collect as little data as possible, and your photos never leave your device.

What Data We Collect

We do not collect personal data. Macrovue does not require an account, does not ask for your name or email, and does not track you across apps or websites.

Camera & Photo Access

Macrovue uses your device's camera to photograph restaurant menus. Here's what happens:

  • Photos stay on your device. Images are never uploaded to our servers or any third-party service.
  • On-device text extraction. Menu text is extracted from the photo using Apple's on-device Vision framework. This happens entirely on your iPhone.
  • Text sent for analysis. Only the extracted text (not the photo) is sent to OpenAI's API for nutritional analysis. This text contains menu item names and descriptions — no personal information.

Location Data

If you use the "Explore Nearby" feature, Macrovue accesses your location to show restaurants near you. Your location data is:

  • Used only to query nearby restaurant information
  • Never stored on our servers
  • Never shared with third parties for advertising or tracking

Location access is optional. Macrovue works without it — you can always scan menus directly with your camera.

Third-Party Services

Macrovue uses the following third-party service:

  • OpenAI API — to analyze extracted menu text and generate nutritional estimates. Only menu text is sent. OpenAI's data usage policy applies to this data. Per OpenAI's API terms, API inputs and outputs are not used to train their models.

In-App Purchases

Macrovue offers an optional Pro subscription for unlimited menu scans. Subscriptions are managed entirely through Apple and can be cancelled anytime in your iPhone's Settings. We do not store payment information — all billing is handled by Apple.

Analytics & Tracking

Macrovue does not use any analytics SDKs, tracking pixels, or advertising frameworks. We do not track your behavior, collect device identifiers, or build user profiles.

Data Storage

Your dietary preferences and goals are stored locally on your device using Apple's standard storage mechanisms. This data is not synced to any server and is deleted when you uninstall the app.

Children's Privacy

Macrovue does not knowingly collect data from children under 13. Since we don't collect personal data from any users, this applies universally.

Changes to This Policy

If we make material changes to this policy, we'll update the "Last updated" date at the top. We recommend checking back occasionally.

Contact

Questions about this privacy policy? Reach us at privacy@macrovue.app.
