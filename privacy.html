Privacy Policy

Effective date: February 8, 2026

1. Overview

Macrovue ("the App") is an iOS application that scans restaurant menus and provides personalized meal recommendations with estimated macronutrient information. This policy explains what data the App accesses, how it is used, and what is shared with third parties.

The short version: Your photos never leave your device. Only extracted menu text is sent to OpenAI for analysis. We don't collect personal data, run analytics, or track you.

2. Data Processed on Your Device

The following data is processed entirely on your iPhone and never transmitted to any server:

  • Menu photos — processed using Apple's on-device Vision framework (OCR). The photo itself is never uploaded or sent anywhere.
  • Dietary goals — stored in local app storage (UserDefaults) on your device.
  • App preferences — settings and consent choices stored locally.

3. Data Sent to Third Parties

When you scan a menu and approve AI analysis, the following data is sent to OpenAI's API:

Data Purpose Recipient
Extracted menu text Generate meal recommendations and macro estimates OpenAI (api.openai.com)
Your dietary goal labels Personalize recommendations to your goals OpenAI (api.openai.com)

This data is sent directly from your device to OpenAI using your own API key. Macrovue does not operate any intermediate servers. We never see, store, or have access to your menu scans or API key.

OpenAI's data handling is governed by their Privacy Policy and Terms of Use. When using the API with your own key, OpenAI states that API data is not used to train their models.

4. Your OpenAI API Key

The App requires you to provide your own OpenAI API key to function. This key is:

  • Stored securely in the iOS Keychain on your device
  • Never transmitted to Macrovue or any server other than OpenAI
  • Used only for direct API calls from your device to OpenAI
  • Deletable at any time from the App's Settings screen

5. What We Do NOT Collect

  • No personal information (name, email, phone number, location)
  • No photos or images
  • No analytics or usage tracking
  • No device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • No crash reports sent to us
  • No accounts, logins, or registration
We do not operate any servers. There is no Macrovue backend, database, or cloud service. The App communicates only with OpenAI's API using your own credentials.

6. Consent

Before any data is sent to OpenAI, the App presents a clear consent screen explaining what will be shared. You must explicitly approve before analysis occurs. You can revoke this consent at any time in the App's Settings, which will prevent any further data transmission.

7. Children's Privacy

Macrovue is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children.

8. Data Deletion

Since all data is stored locally on your device:

  • Delete all data: Delete the App from your device. All locally stored data (preferences, API key) will be removed.
  • Delete your API key: Clear it from the App's Settings screen at any time.
  • Revoke AI consent: Toggle off AI Analysis in Settings to prevent any future data transmission.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date. Continued use of the App constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

10. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at macrovue@proton.me.
