The App

Macrovue is a menu scanning and nutrition recommendation app. It uses your device camera to read restaurant menus, extracts text on-device, and sends that text to OpenAI's AI service for analysis. Recommendations are AI-generated estimates and should not be treated as medical or dietary advice. Always consult a healthcare professional for specific dietary needs.

Free Tier
